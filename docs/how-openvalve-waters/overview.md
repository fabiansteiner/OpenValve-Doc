# Overview

OpenValve is not a timer-based irrigation controller. It does not simply water at a fixed schedule. Instead, it measures the soil moisture and waters when the soil gets too dry.

OpenValve’s watering behaviour is mainly determined by two settings and two setup conditions:

1. **[Opening Threshold](./opening-threshold):** when watering starts
2. **[Additional Time](./additional-time):** how much longer watering continues after water reaches the sensor
3. **[Sensor Placement](./sensor-placement):** where and how OpenValve measures the soil moisture
4. **[Soil Type](./soil-type):** how the soil stores water and how water moves through it
